For the fourth quarter of 2019, RPC reported a $0.07 adjusted loss per share compared to a $0.06 diluted earnings per share in the prior year.
On a sequential basis, RPC's fourth quarter revenues decreased 19.5% to $236 million from $293.2 million in the third quarter due primarily to pronounced seasonally lower activity levels and slightly lower pricing.
During the fourth quarter, we continued to execute our downsizing plans, first disclosed in October, including closing various facilities and scrapping equipment.
During the fourth quarter, we recorded additional impairment and other charges of $10.6 million related primarily to severance and underutilized assets, bringing the total of impairment and other charges to $82.3 million in 2019.
