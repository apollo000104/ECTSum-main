In the second quarter, net sales of $301.4 million increased $71.1 million or 30.9% versus the second quarter of 2020 due to a 23.6% increase in net merchandise sales and 106.8% increase in net service sales.
We delivered strong second quarter sales and profitability growth, with comp sales up 31.3% versus last year and up 10% on a two-year stack basis.
As Charles noted, second quarter 2021 comparable store sales increased 31.3% versus the second quarter of 2020 and 10% on a two-year stack basis.
During the second quarter, we opened four new stores bringing our total store count to 416 at the end of June.
The second quarter of 2021 and 2020 had no non-GAAP adjustments to gross margin.

On a two-year basis, net service sales were up 10%, demonstrating the benefit from our investments in improving the installation customer experience.
As Charles noted, second quarter 2021 comparable store sales increased 31.3% versus the second quarter of 2020 and 10% on a two-year stack basis.
Gross profit increased 27.7% in the second quarter of 2021 to $112.7 million from $88.3 million in the comparable period of 2020, an increase to 10% from $102.5 million in the second quarter of 2019.
The 220 basis point improvement in the second quarter of 2021 adjusted gross margin versus 2019 is particularly impressive given that the cost of merchandise sold on certain products imported from China included 25% tariff rates in the second quarter of 2021 compared to only 10% in the second quarter of 2019.
The 10% year-over-year reduction in inventory was primarily driven by supply chain constraints on replenishment and strong sales that kept inventory below our targeted level.
That said, given the combination of increasingly challenging supply chain constraints on inventory replenishment, potential consumer demand shift and the potential impact of the COVID-19 delta variant, we believe it is prudent to plan for slowing comparable sales on a two-year stack basis for the second half of 2021 compared to the 10% we delivered in the second quarter.
